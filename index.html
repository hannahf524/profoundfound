Profound Found: Markov Ignitions

Found poetry is a type of poetry that is created by taking pieces of text from other sources. Markov chains are able to generate thematically and grammatically plausible text that a poet can then reframe, reorder, and refashion in a way that imparts a new meaning. Computationally generated text, such as that created by Markov chains, can be used as a source for found poetry and serve as a creative "ignition" for poets.
